# Alphabera Global SEO Playbook

## Immediate Priorities

1. Create and verify a Google Business Profile for Alphabera Global.
2. Submit `https://alphabera.global/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console.
3. Request indexing for:
   - homepage
   - Roorkee page
   - Dehradun page
   - Uttarakhand page
   - service pages
   - blog articles

## Local SEO Consistency

- Keep the same business name everywhere: `Alphabera Global`
- Use the same email everywhere: `alphabera.global@gmail.com`
- Keep service areas consistent: `Roorkee`, `Dehradun`, `Uttarakhand`
- If you add a phone number or address publicly, use the same exact format on:
  - website
  - Google Business Profile
  - social pages
  - directory listings

## Reviews

- Ask real clients for Google reviews.
- Encourage reviews that naturally mention:
  - service taken
  - city or region
  - business outcome
- Never buy or fake reviews.

## Local Citations and Links

- Get listed on real business directories and local platforms.
- Pursue backlinks from:
  - local business associations
  - chambers of commerce
  - local blogs and publications
  - client websites
  - event sponsorships or partnerships

## Search Console Routine

- Check indexing weekly.
- Watch performance queries for:
  - `digital marketing agency roorkee`
  - `digital marketing agency dehradun`
  - `seo services roorkee`
  - `website development dehradun`
- Improve titles/meta descriptions for pages getting impressions but low clicks.

## What Not To Do

- No keyword stuffing
- No fake locations
- No doorway pages
- No hidden text
- No fake reviews
- No copied AI spam articles
